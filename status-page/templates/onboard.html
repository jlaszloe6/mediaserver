What to Expect

The Onboarding Process

  • 1 Fill out the form 1 min
  • 2 Wait for admin approval manual
  • 3 Authorize Trakt (2x) 2 min
  • 4 Set up VPN on your device 5 min
  • 5 Install Plex and start watching

Before You Start

  1. Create a free Plex account
  2. Create a free Trakt account
  3. Use the same email for Plex and for this form

What You'll Need After Approval

  • Your phone, laptop, or TV to install WireGuard VPN
  • The Plex app on whatever you watch on
  • Your Trakt account to authorize the connection (done in the browser)

How It Works

  1. Add a movie or show to your Trakt watchlist
  2. The server picks it up within an hour
  3. It downloads and appears in Plex
  4. 30 days after watching, it's auto-removed to free space

You can also browse and request through Seerr, a visual catalog with posters and ratings.

FAQ

How long until content appears?
Up to ~2 hours (hourly sync + download + 15-min Plex scan).
Movies still in theaters?
Add to your watchlist — it downloads once a digital release is out.
How to remove content?
Remove from Trakt watchlist or delete in Plex. Both fully automatic.
Storage limit?
Guests share a quota. Watched content auto-cleans after 30 days.
Why VPN?
The VPN securely connects your device to the server. Plex won't work without it.
Quality?
1080p preferred with English subtitles. 4K only as fallback.
