How It Works

  1. You add a movie or TV show to your Trakt watchlist
  2. The server picks it up automatically (within an hour)
  3. It downloads and appears in Plex, ready to watch
  4. 30 days after you've watched it, it's automatically removed to free up space

You can also browse and request media through Seerr, a visual catalog with posters, ratings, and trailers.

FAQ

How long until something appears in Plex?
The server checks your watchlist every hour, downloads take time, and Plex scans every 15 minutes. Total: up to ~2 hours.

Can I request something still in theaters?
Yes, add it to your watchlist. It won't download until a digital release is available, then it's grabbed automatically.

How do I remove something?
Remove it from your Trakt watchlist — the server will delete it from Plex within ~2 hours. Or delete it directly in Plex.

Is there a storage limit?
Yes, guests share a storage quota. Watched content is automatically cleaned up after 30 days to free space.

Do I need a VPN?
Yes. After approval you'll get a VPN config file to connect securely. Plex won't work without it.

What quality are the downloads?
The server prefers 1080p releases with English subtitles included. 4K is only used as a fallback.
