Media Server

Stream movies and TV shows on demand

How It Works

  1. Add a movie or show to your Trakt watchlist
  2. The server picks it up within an hour
  3. It downloads and appears in Plex, ready to watch
  4. 30 days after watching, it's auto-removed to free space

You can also browse and request through Seerr, a visual catalog with posters and ratings.

1080p preferred with English subtitles. 4K only as fallback.

Guests share a storage quota. Watched content auto-cleans after 30 days.

FAQ

How long until content appears in Plex?
Up to ~2 hours. The server checks your watchlist every hour, downloads take time, and Plex scans for new files every 15 minutes.
Can I request movies still in theaters?
Yes. Add it to your watchlist — it won't download until a digital release is available, then it's grabbed automatically.
How do I remove something?
Remove it from your Trakt watchlist — gone from Plex within ~2 hours. Or delete it directly in Plex — cleaned up within 30 minutes.
Why do I need a VPN?
The VPN securely connects your device to the media server. Without it, Plex can't find the server or stream content.
Why is Trakt authorized twice?
TV shows (Sonarr) and movies (Radarr) use separate systems. Each needs its own Trakt connection, so you authorize once for TV and once for movies.
I finished a show. Do I need to do anything?
No. Watched content is automatically removed after 30 days. Remove it from your Trakt watchlist if you don't want new episodes.

What to Expect

About 10 minutes of your time, plus a wait for admin approval.

  • 1 Fill out the request form 1 min
  • 2 Wait for admin approval manual
  • 3 Authorize Trakt for TV shows 1 min
  • 4 Authorize Trakt for movies 1 min
  • 5 Set up VPN on your device 5 min
  • 6 Install Plex and start watching

After approval, you'll get a link to continue. Everything is guided — no technical knowledge needed.

You'll need: WireGuard VPN app + Plex app on your device. Trakt auth is done in the browser.
